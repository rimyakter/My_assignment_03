Frequently Asked Questions

How can I choose fresh vegetables?

Use your senses to evaluate the produce. Look for firm produce without soft spots, and bright colors.

Can I buy imperfect produce?

Yes, imperfect produce is often just as nutritious and delicious as produce that is perfectly shaped. It may also be less expensive.

What are greenhouse-grown vegetables?

Greenhouse-grown vegetables are available year-round and are consistently fresh.
